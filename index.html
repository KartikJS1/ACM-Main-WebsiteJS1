About Us

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), the world's largest scientific and educational computing society is a US-based international learned society for computing . WCE ACM Student Chapter emerged with the visionary goal of acquainting students with a dynamic educational and scientific Association. WCE ACM Student Chapter, authorized by ACM INDIA COUNCIL on March 18, 2016, aims to impart knowledge and promote innovation in the field of computer science. We embark on a journey where passion converges with purpose, creating a vibrant space for innovation, learning, and unparalleled contributions to the ever-evolving real meaning of technology.
